About Colorado Teens for Vaccines

Colorado Teens for Vaccines operates for charitable and educational purposes as a recognized 501(c)(3) (EIN 87-1704832).

Specific purpose: to empower teens and families to make informed vaccination decisions through education, access to clinics, and peer‑led advocacy. We collaborate with schools, clinics, and youth groups statewide.

  • We do not provide medical advice; individuals should consult healthcare professionals.
  • No substantial part of our activities involves lobbying or political campaigning.
  • Assets are irrevocably dedicated to charitable and educational purposes; upon dissolution they pass to a 501(c)(3).
