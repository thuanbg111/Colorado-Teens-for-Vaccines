Colorado Teens for Vaccines

We empower teens and families to make informed vaccination decisions through education, access to clinics, and peer‑led advocacy. Our work centers on evidence‑based information, youth leadership, and partnerships with clinics so every teen can access recommended vaccines.

Learn more See programs

What We Do

Give teens the facts, the support, and the access they need.

  • School & community presentations with science‑backed resources
  • Pop‑up vaccine clinics with trusted medical partners
  • Peer Ambassador program to combat myths and boost uptake

Education

Clear, age‑appropriate content aligned with CDC/ACIP guidelines.

Clinics

On‑site and pop‑up vaccination events with sign‑ups and reminders.

Peer Ambassadors

Train teens to be health advocates in their schools and communities.
